    Block chain and Cryptocurrencies:

    Firstly, Cryptocurrency is an unregulated digital currency and is only available in electronic form. It can only be stored and traded through designated computer or software applications, mobile apps, or through a dedicated digital wallet. Some names of cryptocurrencies such as bitcoin, Ethereum, Chainlink. Moreover, all of the above coins are operated on a block chain system and it can be traded as real money out there. Cryptocurrencies can be traded through dedicated and secure networks. In addition, Cryptocurrencies are traded on the block chain network. It also brings new benefits to today's technology industry and the economy. Cryptocurrencies offer the opportunity for entrepreneurs or international parties to conduct one-to-one online exchanges without the complications and additional fees that are often associated with relevant international currency exchanges. to a third party. On the other hand, Block chain is the platform that brings electronic money. it is the foundation for creating vehicles of the transaction and enabling the transfer of value and information. The first block chain was invented and designed by Satoshi Nakamoto in 2008 and realized the following year as a core part of Bitcoin, as block chain technology served as a ledger for all deal. Moreover, Block chains serve as the fundamental underlying technology, of which cryptocurrencies are a part of the ecosystem. They go hand in hand, and cryptocurrencies are often needed to transact on a block chain. But without block chain, we wouldn't have the means for these transactions to be recorded and executed. Cryptocurrencies are traded based on block chain technology, each cryptocurrency transaction is a unique exchange between two parties and protecting users from problems such as identity theft. Besides the current features that block chains and cryptocurrencies can offer, there are many uses beyond cryptocurrencies. Block chains are being used to speed cancer research, improve healthcare record sharing and verify people's identities. As an illustration, a large amount of today's top regulators admit that Block chain plays an important role in the success of the banking industry and financial companies, and in the future, will consider using block chain instead. for the current bank transfer system. Not only provide faster, more cost-effective payment methods. Block chain also helps eliminate intermediaries in transactions, allows people to work directly with each other, improving efficiency in sending and receiving electronic coins.     Overview: Overall, this will be a smart and automatic watering project. This project will include the installation of a sprinkler system and temperature sensor system for each area separately. Moreover, this is not only a simple automatic watering project but also includes an analysis of the plant's condition and its growth environment. It will help optimize plant growth and achieve the same results on a large scale. Because of that, such benefits save labor, time and quantity of production will be significantly increased. This will be a technology suitable for Vietnam's environment, it will help farmers and industry of Vietnam to achieve success in the future.

    Description: A smart and automatic watering project will have 2 main steps which are to set irrigation and ambient sensor systems. Firstly, the irrigation system will be distributed correctly in each area so that when watering will ensure the amount of water to water the plants in an equal amount. For instance, the installation of an automatic irrigation system for orange trees will be the position between the 4 trees, it will help when watering those 4 orange trees will have an equal amount of water and at the same time of day. Moreover, even if a farm is guaranteed that every tree will have the same watering regime, the quality of the oranges will always be the same and guaranteed. Besides orange trees, all such agricultural produce as vegetables, potatoes and even ornamental plants will have similar results.

    Secondly, ambient sensor systems will also be installed based on application calculations. This system will be placed high above and around the habitat of these trees and all other surrounding areas. Moreover, their task will be to collect information about temperature, light, humidity and wind to give information about the center of the application. Accordingly, according to the data that the application has collected, the programmed system will analyze the status of the trees in each area, which will result in how to provide suitable and timely solutions for trees. On the other hand, after receiving information and analysis from ambient sensor systems, there will be a programmed program for the watering system to adjust the amount of water accordingly (this amount of water will include fertilizer dissolve in water to come). For instance, on dry days, ambient sensor systems will notify the system's main center that the orange tree area is in need of water and this dry environment will directly affect the tree. Based on the analytical information, the irrigation system will be adjusted to irrigate with more water and appropriate fertilizer to help stabilize the orange trees.

    Finally, once the system is complete, its owners can directly view the status of the plants through analysis and reports of ambient sensor systems. Moreover, the user will not need to go directly to the planting area to control the device because the system will be integrated on the system's computer and will grow to be compatible with the phone if the user is not directly located at the computer-controlled position. For example, when the system owner does not need to go to the location of each tree in hot weather but instead in the control area to check everything on the data. In addition, when they have a job they cannot stay on the farm and have to go to another city, they can still connect to the device by phone to control and check the situation of the tree. In short, an intelligent and automatic watering project will be installed with all the information based on the most accurate experts to ensure that there will be no errors in interpreting and adjusting the water volume for suitable.
